# How are you DevOps?

**Type**: Group work

**Can be used**: For your examproject.

---

## The assignment

Inspired by [The DevOps Handbook]() start writing down arguments for why you are DevOps.

Also write down what keeps you from being fully DevOps while reflecting on why. 

You can use this matrix [**Rubrik: How DevOps Are You?**](Rubrik_How_DevOps_Are_You.md) as a checklist for making sure all areas are covered.
